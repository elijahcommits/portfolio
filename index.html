Who I am

from Washougal, Washington

My name is Elijah, but my friends call me Eli. I'm a jack-of-all-trades, and for some reason I am still (obsessively) learning more things. I was a late bloomer in some senses, but in others I was very early. Before high school, I had a lot of promise. Once in the 8th grade, I raised $4200 for food baskets for families in need. This is truly my greatest accomplishment, so far

I taught myself to read with no help, and in kindergarten I was sent to 3rd grade for reading. In 4th grade, I was told I was reading at a college level. This was not a good thing. School came so easily for me, that I never needed to, or learned to study. Not until more recently, at least.

Presently, I'm a whore for learning. I switch between subjects like a polygamist does partners. Right now, I'm learning Svelte and Postgres/SQL, but a few months ago I was obsessed with cryptocurrency. A couple years before that, I was so obsessed with psychedelics and dissociatives that I wound up in the psych ward. If you can hold my attention, you'll have a dedicated and very, very hard worker, who will sometimes stay up till 5am (or all night) to finish your project.

Oh, I wasn't just a nerd in school. I was also into basketball, baseball, soccer, tennis, golf, and wrestling. Basically, everything but football, but that's only because my dad was afraid of me getting a concussion.

This is my life in a nutshell.
